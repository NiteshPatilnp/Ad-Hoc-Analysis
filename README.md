## Project Overview
AtliQ Hardware is a consumer electronics brand operating across multiple regions and sales channels. This project analyzes its sales dataset to understand performance, optimize discounting, strengthen product strategy, and improve customer engagement.

---

## Problem Statement
AtliQ Hardware needed a detailed examination of its sales data to detect patterns, trends, and improvement areas across markets, channels, and product categories. Such insights were essential for making informed decisions and staying competitive.

---

## Key Objectives
1. Understand evolving customer and market behaviors.  
2. Measure product performance across various divisions.  
3. Assess how discounting influences overall sales.  
4. Identify sales patterns and seasonality.  
5. Deliver data-backed recommendations to improve revenue and customer satisfaction.

---

##  Dataset Description
The project uses several tables:

1. **dim_customer** – Contains customer-related data  
2. **dim_product** – Contains product-related data  
3. **fact_gross_price** – Contains gross price information for each product  
4. **fact_manufacturing_cost** – Contains the cost incurred in the production of each product  
5. **fact_pre_invoice_deductions** – Contains pre-invoice deductions information for each product  
6. **fact_sales_monthly** – Contains monthly sales data for each product

## Tools & Technologies
- **SQL**: For querying and analyzing data.

---

## Data Analysis Workflow
1. **Data Collection**: Gathered sales records from multiple sources.  
2. **Data Cleaning**: Performed consistency and quality checks using Excel.  
3. **Exploratory Data Analysis**: Identified trends and anomalies via SQL queries.  
4. **Visualization**: Designed clear, interactive visuals in Power BI.  
5. **Insights Generation**: Interpreted findings to guide business direction.  
6. **Recommendations**: Suggested improvement strategies based on insights.

---

## Key Insights
- **Customer & Market Performance**: Recognized leading markets and customers driving revenue growth.  
- **Product Analysis**: Notebooks and accessories performed best; unique product count rose by 36%.  
- **Sales Channel Trends**: Retailers contributed 73% of gross sales in FY2021.  
- **Discount Patterns**: Flipkart, Viveks, Ezone, Croma, and Amazon received the highest average discounts.  
- **Seasonal Patterns**: Q1 2020 (Sept–Nov) saw the highest overall sold quantity.

---

## Recommendations
1. Expand product lines in regions showing strong growth.  
2. Reassess discount strategies to improve margins.  
3. Strengthen investment in the retailer sales channel.  
4. Align marketing and promotions with seasonal demand peaks.

---

## Impact of This Analysis
This analysis provided AtliQ Hardware with clear, actionable insights to enhance sales, improve profitability, and increase customer satisfaction. It also demonstrated the value of storytelling with data and aligning insights with business goals.
